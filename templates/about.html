About BAHON

Simplifying bus travel across Bangladesh since 2020

Our Story

BAHON was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make bus travel in Bangladesh easier, more convenient, and more accessible for everyone. What started as a small venture has now grown into the country's leading bus ticketing platform.

Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of booking bus tickets in Bangladesh - long queues, uncertainty about schedules, and the hassle of visiting multiple counters to find available seats. They envisioned a solution that would bring the entire bus ticketing ecosystem online.

Today, BAHON connects millions of travelers with hundreds of bus operators across the country, offering a seamless booking experience from the comfort of your home or on the go.

Our Mission

To transform the bus travel experience in Bangladesh by providing a reliable, transparent, and convenient ticketing platform that connects travelers with bus operators nationwide.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted travel companion for every journey in Bangladesh and beyond, setting new standards for convenience, reliability, and customer satisfaction in the transportation industry.
