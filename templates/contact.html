Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a bus ticket?

Booking a bus ticket on BAHON is simple. Just search for your desired route, select your travel date, choose your preferred bus and seats, provide passenger details, and complete the payment. You'll receive your e-ticket via email and SMS.

Can I cancel my ticket?

Yes, you can cancel your ticket through your account or by contacting our support team. Cancellation fees may apply depending on how close to the departure time you cancel. Please refer to our refund policy for more details.

How do I get my ticket after booking?

After successful booking, your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email address and phone number via SMS. You can also view and download your ticket from your account on our website or mobile app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile banking (bKash, Nagad), and internet banking. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I book a ticket for someone else?

Yes, you can book tickets for family, friends, or anyone else. Just make sure to enter their correct details during the booking process as these will be verified during boarding.

Still have questions? Check our complete FAQ page or contact us directly.
